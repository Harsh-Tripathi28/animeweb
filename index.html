About Sage Mode

A shinobi learns to borrow
nature's own chakra.

Sage Mode isn't summoned in anger — it's earned in silence. A shinobi sits motionless, breath slowed to nothing, gathering natural energy from the world without losing themselves to it. Move too fast, feel too much, and the balance breaks.

The reward is unmistakable: eye markings sharpen, pupils narrow, the air seems to hum. The fighter finally moves like they belong to the world they're protecting.
